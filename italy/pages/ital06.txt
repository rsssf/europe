== Italy 2005/06

First Level (Serie A)

<!-- $table1$ -->


[*] played home matches in Padova until round 8

NB: after the match-fixing scandal the following verdicts were reached
    after appeals were heard on Jul 25, 2006:
    Juventus are stripped from their 2004/05 and 2005/06 scudetti, are
      demoted to last place 2005/06 and relegated to Serie B where they
      will start 2006/07 with -17 points, reprieving Messina;
    Inter were declared champions on Jul 26, 2006;
    Milan have 30 points deducted for 2005/06 and start 2006/07 with -8;
    Fiorentina have 30 points deducted for 2005/06 and start 2006/07 with -19;
    Lazio have 30 points deducted for 2005/06 and start 2006/07 with -11;
    Reggina will start 2006/07 with -15.
