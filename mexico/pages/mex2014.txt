an additional bonus point; if the series was tied after 2 matches, the team with more away goals earn the extra point; if still tied no bonus point was awarded.

▪ Group Stage ▪

The Group Stage was played in 3 home/away series, with the winners of each series
earning an additional bonus point; if the series was tied after 2 matches, the
team with more away goals earn the extra point; if still tied no bonus point was
awarded.
