Atlas, as the winner of the Apertura 2021 and Clausura 2022 tournaments,
automatically won the title of "Campeón de Campeones", however, due to
commercial commitments in Liga MX, an "official" match must be played
in the United States as the kickoff of the new season 2022-23.

SuperCopa LMX game between Cruz Azul (Campeón de Campeones 2020-21)
and Atlas (Campeón de Campeones 2021-22) was introduced.
