== Mexico 2004/05

Primera División Nacional

Apertura 2004
=============

NB: for 2004/05 the league is reduced to 18 clubs; Gallos Blancos
    and Irapuato were excluded, after a vote among the club owners
    which was survived by Puebla and Santos (the other two candidates
    for exclusion).  Queretaro will play at the 2nd level 2004/05
    while Irapuato (who were also robbed of their franchise at the
    end of 2000/01 when their team was moved to Veracruz) will not
    play at all next season.
