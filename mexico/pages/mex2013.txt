NB: the Cup Tournament is played by 14 teams from the Liga MX (first level) and 14 teams
    from the Ascenso MX (second level);
    Monterrey, Tigres, Santos and Guadalajara from Liga MX excluded because of participating
    in the CONCACAF Champions League 2012-13;
     Cruz Azul Hidalgo (Ascenso MX) (last in the 2012 Clausura tournament) also excluded

▪ Group Stage ▪

The Group Stage was played in 3 home/away series, with the winners of each series earning
an additional bonus point; if the series was tied after 2 matches, the team with more away goals
earn the extra point; if still tied no bonus point was awarded.

==== Copa MX Clausura 2013  ‹§copac›

‹Group Stage, see §copacgrp› | ‹Playoff Stage, see §copacplay›


NB: the Cup Tournament is played by 11 teams from the Liga MX (first level) and 13 teams
    from the Ascenso MX (second level);
    Monterrey, Tigres, Santos and Guadalajara from Liga MX excluded because of participating
    in the CONCACAF Champions League 2012-13;
    Toluca, Tijuana and León also excluded because of participating in Copa Libertadores 2013
    Pumas Morelos and Leones Negros (Ascenso MX) (last in the 2012 Apertura tournament) excluded too.

▪ Group Stage ▪


The Group Stage was played in 3 home/away series, with the winners of each series earning
an additional bonus point; if the series was tied after 2 matches, the team with more away goals
earn the extra point; if still tied no bonus point was awarded.
